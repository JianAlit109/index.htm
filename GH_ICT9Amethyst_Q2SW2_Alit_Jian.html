Breed

Description

Pug

It is a squarely built muscular dog with a height of 10 to 13 inches and weighs about 14 to 18 pounds at maturity. Most people value this breed as it is loyal and vigilant.

Pembroke Welsh Corgi

This breed is outgoing and friendly. It weighs 24 to 30 pounds and stands at 10 to 12 inches tall.

Samoyed

This breed is known for its soft fur and playful characteristic. Its height span is usually 19 to 23.5 inches and weighs 35 to 65 pounds.
